Cosine Similarity

Similarity measures are used to determine how many features two entities have in common.

The Cosine Similarity is a measure of the angle between the feature vectors of two nodes. The features used to determine similarity with cosine similarity are a node's neighbours, including the direction, types and weight of the transactions connecting them.
